# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
HR Analytics Dashboard providing key workforce insights through attrition analysis, demographic segmentation, gender trends, and job satisfaction distribution. This dashboard empowers HR leaders to identify high-risk groups, understand employee behavior, and make data-driven decisions to improve retention and workplace engagement.

# Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides an interactive and insightful overview of workforce metrics including attrition, demographics, job satisfaction, and department-wise employee distribution.
The dashboard enables HR leaders to identify areas of concern, understand workforce behavior, and take data-driven actions to improve retention and employee experience.

This visualization highlights key workforce KPIs and presents attrition trends with breakdowns by age, gender, job role, department, and education field.

# Dashboard Features & Visuals

| Insight Area                | Visual Type          | Purpose                                                |
| --------------------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Workforce Overview          | KPI Cards            | Display total employees, attrition rate & demographics |
| Department-wise Attrition   | Pie Chart            | Identify departments with highest turnover             |
| Employee Age Distribution   | Bar Chart            | Analyze dominant age groups in workforce               |
| Job Satisfaction Rating     | Heatmap              | Compare satisfaction levels across job roles           |
| Education Field & Attrition | Horizontal Bar Chart | Understand retention issues by qualification           |
| Gender, Age & Attrition     | Donut Charts         | Observe gender-based attrition across age cohorts      |
